# Portfolio-level security

The security overview page shows a summary of findings and estimated severity. From left to right, the tiles read as follows:

<img width="1685" height="861" alt="New_portfolio_security " src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4c82cdb3-cf36-4288-a5b9-c4815e742c85" />


* *Systems and Findings*: the totals of systems being scanned and the sum of identified Findings. Below those counts, it shows the total changes over the selected time period. A mouseover shows an elaboration:

<img width="373" height="210" alt="new_systems_findings" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1efe3e7c-386f-419c-a32f-9cfaa71dadd5" />


* *Systems per CVSS severity*: the count of systems where their highest-risk finding is in these risk categories, i.e. <img src="../images/system-security-icon-low1.png" class="inline" /> *Low*, <img src="../images/system-security-icon-medium1.png" class="inline" /> *Medium*, <img src="../images/system-security-icon-high1.png" class="inline" /> *High*, or <img src="../images/system-security-icon-critical1.png" class="inline" /> *Critical*. A mouseover on the colored bar shows the exact count:

<img width="341" height="194" alt="New_cvss_severity" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c6d789ff-fb46-461d-8816-c2c40eedc61a" />


* *Open and resolved findings*: the count of *New*, *Existing* and *Resolved* findings over the selected time period in the top right corner data selector. Note that the sums for *New* and *Resolved* are the same totals as shown in the *Findings* tile on the left. A mouseover on the colored bar shows the exact count:

<img width="342" height="195" alt="New_open resolved" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c3829814-6698-4fda-a39c-0717ecea10ac" />


* *Security objectives overview*: shows the status of set security goals on portfolio level, as below.

<img width="344" height="193" alt="New_security_objectives" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6453441f-d76b-4d64-ae08-ec677c1e1e19" />


In the bottom part of the screen, each system is shown with a summary of its counts: number of findings per *CVSS* risk category and *Finding age* counts. For larger portfolios it may be useful to sort these on different characteristics. 


Sorting can be done per columns (here, the total number of "*Critical risk*" findings as an example). The top right bottom for exporting the data as a spreadsheet may be useful for further analysis.
